## 🎨 Color Scheme

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: Dark slate (#0f172a)
- **Cards**: Slate (#1e293b)
- **Primary**: Blue (#2563eb)
- **Success**: Green (#16a34a)
- **Warning**: Yellow (#eab308)
- **Error**: Red (#dc2626)

### Text Colors
- **Primary**: White (#ffffff)
- **Secondary**: Light slate (#cbd5e1)
- **Tertiary**: Slate (#94a3b8)
- **Disabled**: Dark slate (#475569)

### Interactive States
- **Hover**: Lighter shade
- **Active**: Primary color
- **Disabled**: Grey with reduced opacity
- **Focus**: Blue border

## 📐 Layout Specifications

### Spacing
- **Page padding**: 16px (px-4)
- **Section gap**: 24px (gap-6)
- **Card padding**: 12px (p-3)
- **Button padding**: 12px 16px (px-4 py-3)

### Typography
- **Heading 1**: 24px, bold
- **Heading 2**: 20px, bold
- **Heading 3**: 18px, semibold
- **Body**: 14px, regular
- **Small**: 12px, regular

### Border Radius
- **Cards**: 12px (rounded-xl)
- **Buttons**: 8px (rounded-lg)
- **Chips**: 9999px (rounded-full)
- **Inputs**: 12px (rounded-xl)

### Shadows
- **Cards**: subtle shadow
- **Modals**: medium shadow
- **Floating**: large shadow

## 🎭 Animations

### Transitions
- **Duration**: 300ms
- **Easing**: ease-out
- **Properties**: all, transform, opacity

### Hover Effects
- **Scale**: 1.02 (cards)
- **Scale**: 1.05 (buttons)
- **Opacity**: 0.8 (icons)

### Loading States
- **Skeleton**: pulse animation
- **Spinner**: rotate animation
- **Fade**: opacity transition

## 📱 Responsive Breakpoints

### Mobile (default)
- **Grid**: 2 columns
- **Font**: base size
- **Spacing**: compact

### Tablet (768px+)
- **Grid**: 3 columns
- **Font**: slightly larger
- **Spacing**: comfortable

### Desktop (1024px+)
- **Grid**: 4 columns
- **Font**: larger
- **Spacing**: spacious
- **Max width**: 1280px
